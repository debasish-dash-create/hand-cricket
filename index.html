2V2 TACTICS

  • TEAM PLAY: 2 Players vs 2 Players. 4 Overs Total.
  • STRIKE ROTATION: Scoring 1, 3, or 5 runs rotates the strike to your partner.
  • OVER ROTATION: The bowler and striker both rotate at the end of every 6-ball over.
  • WICKETS: Each team has 2 Wickets. If a player gets out, the next player bats until both are out.

RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

  • THE TOSS: A coin flip decides who bats or bowls. (Synced online).
  • THE CLOCK: You have exactly 5 SECONDS to select a number (1-6).
  • AFK PENALTY: Miss the timer? It's a dot ball (0). 3 consecutive misses = OUT!
  • SCORING: If your number differs from the bowler's, batsman scores that number.
  • WICKETS: 1 wicket match! If numbers match, batsman is OUT.
